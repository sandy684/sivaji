1. What is the boarding time for flights?

Boarding usually starts 45 to 60 minutes before departure and closes 15–20 minutes before take-off. Check your boarding pass for exact details.

2. What happens if I arrive late at the airport?

If you arrive late and miss check-in or boarding, you may not be allowed to board and may have to rebook your flight at your own expense.

3. How long is the security and immigration process? at a time?

Security checks typically take 15–30 minutes. Immigration at international terminals may take 30–60 minutes depending on queue size.

4. Can I check in online before my flight?

Yes, most airlines allow online check-in 24–48 hours before departure

5. How long does the visa process take?

Visa processing time varies by country. It usually takes 3 to 15 working days, but some embassies may take longer.

6. Is visa approval guaranteed if I apply through your agency?

No, visa decisions are made solely by embassies or consulates. We help you prepare your application properly to increase your chances.

7. What if my visa is rejected?

We will help you understand the reason for rejection and assist in reapplying if possible.
