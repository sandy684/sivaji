Russia Visa – General Document Requirements
  • Original passport with a minimum validity of 6 months and at least 3 blank pages, including all previous passports if applicable.
  • Completed and signed visa application form.
  • Two recent color photographs with a white background and matte finish (as per photo specifications).
  • A personal covering letter detailing the purpose of your travel to the destination country.
  • Original bank statement for the last 6 months, stamped and updated with the bank's official seal.
  • Proof of return flight tickets showing travel from and back to your home country.
  • Tourist confirmation letter issued by a Russian tour operator registered in the Unified Federal Register of Tour Operators with a unique reference number.
  • Important: The government enforces strict photo requirements; please ensure your photographs meet the specified criteria.
Russia Visa – Additional Requirements Based on Applicant Type
    • If Employed:
      • Leave approval letter on company letterhead with an official seal.
      • Salary slips for the past 3 months.
    • If Self-Employed:
      • Business registration documents such as License, Memorandum of Association, or Partnership Deed.
      • Company’s bank statements for the last 6 months, stamped by the bank.
    • If Retired:
      • Proof of retirement such as pension book or pension statement.
    • If Student:
      • Valid student ID card from school, college, or educational institution.
    • If Minor:
      • Birth certificate showing the names of both parents.
      • Notarized letter of consent (NOC).
      • If traveling with one parent: consent letter legalized by the non-traveling parent.
      • If traveling alone or without both parents: notarized consent from both parents.
      • Death certificate of parent(s), if applicable.
      • Identity proof of both parents.
    • If Visiting Friends or Relatives:
      • Invitation letter detailing the relationship and purpose of visit.
      • Inviter’s ID proof such as passport or residence permit.
      • Address proof of inviter, e.g., utility bill.
    • If Sponsored:
      • Sponsorship letter outlining the visitor's name, relationship, reason for visit, intended duration, travel dates, and any other relevant details.
      • Sponsor’s ID proof: passport or residence permit.
      • Address proof of sponsor: utility bill.
      • Evidence of financial support: bank statements and salary slips.
    • For Business Visa:
      • Invitation letter from the Russian company (INN Letter) or one issued by the Main Directorate for Migration Affairs in Russia.
      • Business covering letter from the Indian company on official letterhead.
