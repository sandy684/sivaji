UK Visa – General Document Requirements
  • Valid original passport with at least 6 months of validity and a minimum of three blank pages, along with all previous passports (if available).
  • Completed online UK visa application form.
  • Visa appointment confirmation letter.
  • Proof of UK visa fee payment (official receipt).
  • Provide two up-to-date coloured photographs that meet the official photo requirements.(contact us)
  • Personal covering letter – on plain paper for employed applicants or on company letterhead for self-employed individuals.
  • Original bank statement for the last 6 months showing sufficient financial balance.
  • Income Tax Returns or Form 16 for the past 3 years.
  • Additional supporting financial documents like fixed deposits, property ownership proof, recurring deposits, or other investments (optional but recommended).
UK Visa – Additional Requirements Based on Applicant Type
  • For Employed Applicants: Leave approval letter from the employer with official seal and signature, along with the last 3 months’ salary slips.
  • For Self-Employed Individuals: Business registration documents such as MOA or partnership deed, company’s bank statement for the last 6 months, and IT returns for the past 3 years.
  • For Retired Persons: Proof of retirement, such as a pension book or recent pension statement.
  • For Students: Valid student ID card and a bonafide certificate from the school, college, or institution.
  • For Minors: Birth certificate, notarized No Objection Certificate (NOC) on ₹100 stamp paper from parents/non-accompanying parent(s), and parent’s ID proof (Passport or PAN card).
  • If Visiting Friends or Relatives: Invitation letter from the host, a copy of the host’s ID proof (Passport or Resident Permit), and address proof such as an electricity or utility bill.
  • If Sponsored: Sponsorship letter, sponsor’s valid national ID (Passport, PAN card, or Resident Permit), latest 6 months’ bank statement, and income tax returns for the past 3 years.
  • For Business Visa Applicants: Invitation letter from the hosting company outlining the trip purpose and business details, and a covering letter from the Indian company printed on official letterhead.
