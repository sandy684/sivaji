Japan Visa – General Document Requirements
  • The following are the requirements for applying for a visa to Australia:
  • Valid original passport with at least 6 months validity and a minimum of 3 blank pages, along with any previous passports, if available.
  • A notarized photocopy of the complete current passport, including the biodata page, the last page, and any amendment pages.
  • Duly completed Australian visa application form.
  • A personal covering letter – on plain paper for employed individuals and on company letterhead for self-employed applicants.
  • Two recent colour passport-size photographs that meet the official photo specifications.
  • Original bank statement for the last 6 months showing adequate financial balance.
  • Income Tax Returns (ITR) or Form 16 for the past three assessment years.
  • Additional financial documents such as fixed deposits, property ownership proof, or investment records (optional but recommended).
  • Medical examinations, including chest x-ray tests, may be required as part of the application process.
  • Please be aware: Photograph guidelines are strictly enforced; ensure your photos fully comply with the required specifications.
Japan Visa – Additional Requirements Based on Applicant Type
  • For Employees: Leave approval letter from the employer (signed and stamped with the company seal), along with the latest 3 months’ salary slips, confirming employment and granted leave during the travel period.
  • For Self-Employed Individuals: Proof of business ownership such as Business Registration Certificate, Memorandum of Association (MOA), or Partnership Deed; updated company bank statements for the last 6 months showing active transactions; and income tax returns for the last 3 years to establish financial stability.
  • For Retired Applicants: Retirement proof documents like a pension passbook, pension payment statements, or a retirement order to verify retired status and source of income.
  • For Students: Valid student identification card from the academic institution and a recent bonafide certificate confirming current enrollment and granting permission for international travel.
  • For Minors: Birth certificate to establish the minor’s identity and age, a notarized No Objection Certificate (NOC) on ₹100 stamp paper from both parents or the non-accompanying parent(s), and copies of their government-issued ID (Passport or PAN card) to confirm consent for travel.
  • When Visiting Friends/Relatives: An original invitation letter from the host, a copy of their valid identification document (such as passport or residency permit), and a recent utility bill (e.g., electricity or water bill) as proof of the host’s residential address.
  • If Sponsored: A formal sponsorship letter from the sponsor, a copy of their national ID (Passport, PAN card, or resident permit), the sponsor's bank statements for the past 6 months reflecting financial capacity, and income tax returns for the last 3 years.
  • For Business Visa: An official invitation letter from the host company clearly stating the purpose of the visit and business details; if the foreign company is covering the expenses, an original Letter of Guarantee and a certified copy of the business registration certificate are required. Additionally, a covering letter from the Indian company printed on official company letterhead must be provided.
