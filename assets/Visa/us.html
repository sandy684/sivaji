US Visa – General Document Requirements
  • Valid original passport with a minimum of six months’ validity and at least three blank pages, including all previous passports if applicable.
  • Printed confirmation page of your DS-160 visa application form.
  • Appointment confirmation letter for the visa interview.
  • Receipt for the US visa fee payment.
  • Provide two up-to-date coloured photographs that meet the official photo requirements.(contact us)
  • Personal cover letter—on plain paper for employed applicants or on company letterhead for self-employed individuals.
  • Original, updated bank statements from the last six months demonstrating sufficient funds.
  • Income tax returns or Form 16 for the past three years.
  • Additional financial documents such as fixed deposits, property ownership proofs, recurring deposits, or other investments (optional but recommended).
US Visa – Additional Requirements Based on Applicant Type
  • If Employed: Original leave approval certificate with company seal and authorized signature; salary slips for the last three months.
  • If Self-Employed: Business registration documents such as Business License, MOA, or Partnership Deed; company bank statements from the past six months; company income tax returns for the last three years.
  • If Retired: Proof of retirement such as pension book or pension statements.
  • If Student: Valid student ID card from school, college, or institute; bonafide certificate from the educational institution.
  • If Minor: Birth certificate; notarized No Objection Certificate (NOC) from parents or non-accompanying parent on ₹100 stamp paper; parent’s ID proof such as passport or PAN card.
  • If Visiting Friends or Relatives: Invitation letter from the host; copy of host’s ID proof (passport or residency permit); proof of address such as electricity or utility bill.
  • If Sponsored: Sponsorship letter; sponsor’s valid national ID (passport, PAN card, or residency permit); updated bank statements for the last six months; income tax returns for the past three years.
  • For US Business Visa: Invitation letter from the host company outlining the purpose of the trip and business details; covering letter from the Indian company on official letterhead.
