Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cancellation by the Customer

  • Flight Tickets: Once a flight ticket is booked and confirmed, cancellations are subject to the airline’s policies. You may incur cancellation fees or may not be eligible for a refund depending on the airline’s terms.
  • Visa Processing Services: Visa application services once initiated cannot be canceled or refunded. The application fee is non-refundable, even if the visa is not approved by the respective embassy or consulate.

Refund Policy

  • Flight Tickets: Refunds for canceled flights are subject to airline policies. Non-refundable tickets will not be refunded. For refundable tickets, a cancellation fee may apply.
  • Visa Processing Fees: Fees for visa processing are non-refundable after submission of the application, regardless of the outcome of the visa application.
  • Refund Processing Time: Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within 7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Cancellations by the Airline

  • If your flight is canceled by the airline, you will be offered an alternative flight or a full refund, subject to the airline’s policy.
  • We will notify you as soon as we are informed of any flight cancellations or changes, but the final decision regarding refunds and rebooking rests with the airline.

Changes to Bookings

  • Changes to flight bookings or visa details (e.g., name correction, flight date change) are subject to availability and may incur additional charges or penalties as per airline or visa service policies.
  • Changes must be requested at least 74 hours before the scheduled departure for flight bookings and as per visa processing timelines.
